Single notification container, with multiple types of notifications

  • Alert
  • Status
  • each notification contains a child aria-live region and the notifications are injected and removed from the DOM in their entirety as they are launched and dismissed
  • uses js to manage focus with event.preventDefault()
  • using href="#" or href="#navbarSubmenuID" exhibit same behavior
  • focus index remains on the submenu toggle upon expanding dropdown, unless we programatically shift focus to something else
